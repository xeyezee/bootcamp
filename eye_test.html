For the best accuracy (and to prevent memorazation), have someone assist yiu when testing your vision with this eye chart. If you use eyeglasses or contact lenses for driving or other distance vision tasks, wear them during the test.

  1. Place the chart on a wall easel 10 feet away.
  2. Cover one eye with your hand, a large spoon or some other item that completely blocks the vision of the covered eye.
  3. Identify the line on the chart you cancomfotably read. Read the letters on that line aloud. Have your assistant stand near the chart and record your accuracy.
  4. Switch to the other eye and repeat.

This is a pretend eye examination.
